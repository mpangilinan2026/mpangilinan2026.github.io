Every Color, Every Hue

Represents a play style for me and you!

White

Morality. Law. Stability. White is a defensive color. It's known for small but plenty creatures, defensive spells and enchants, and heal cantrips. Keep yourself safe and chip away at the opponent!

Green

Harmony. Growth. Ramp. Green is a laid back color. It's known for creatures of different sizes, growth spells, and mana accumulation. Stall out for the late game and get your big creatures to swing for the win!

Blue

Foresight. Planning. Insight. Blue is a reactive color. It's known for evasive little creatures, card draw cantrips, and counterspells. Stall out the game by foiling your opponents plans and win with tempo!

Red

Power. Impulse. Chaos. Red is THE aggressive color. It's known for small creatures, cheap player-damaging spells, and quickplay. Get your spells out fast and close the game out even faster!

Black
